About This Boilerplate

💡
Design tokens: All colors, fonts, spacing, and transitions live in :root at the top of the <style> block. Change them once — every component updates globally.

Add a page: (1) Copy a <section class="view"> block, give it a unique id="view-yourId". (2) Add { id: 'yourId', label: 'Your Page', icon: '🆕' } to the VIEWS array in the script. (3) The router auto-creates nav buttons — or add them manually to match.
